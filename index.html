for the people accountable

When management asks what happened — the answer is already written.

AI at work today is unaccountable. Actions leave no receipts, approvals live in someone's DM history, and the lessons evaporate with the session — so pilots stall in review, or agents run as shadow ops nobody can audit. AccInt is built the other way around: every action a receipt, every approval a held decision with a name and a timestamp, every lesson credited to memory.
